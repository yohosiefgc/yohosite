About Me

Dawn Hosie // Yohosie

Hi there – I’m Dawn Hosie, aka Yohosie. I am a graphic designer, project manager, creative director, and manager. I am a doer and creator, with a hands-on approach to art, content creation, development, and game design. My verticals are exclusively marketing and marketing content. I know how to sell and advertise a product, and create content that is both data-driven and visually appealing.

In addition to my design skills, I have storied experience in project management and team leadership. I believe in equipping artists, developers, QA, and operations with what they need: efficient processes, honest and thoughtful critique, an inclusive space, and an open ear. This is done both through managing individuals, but also managing the work.

My experience spans throughout all verticals and visual media, but I specialize in (1) playable ad units, and (2) creating interactive, static, and video content for the gaming industry. Being able to fuse game design, art, and marketing in my professional life is a fantastic privilege, and every day working in this industry is a joy.

Outside of work, I’m a massive nerd. I am a former professional fighting game player, an RPG enjoyer, a board game dork, and a card game dweeb. Art and gaming are truly my life.

In short, I believe in getting things done, and I get them done to a very high bar of quality and professionalism.
